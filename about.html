About DST

Founded in 1975 by Bill Southard, P.E., DST Controls is a full service control systems and industrial data integration company that serves customers on five continents with on-spec, on-budget, and on-time project delivery.

DST's core offer is automated control and monitoring of industrial equipment, processes, and data. As a control and data systems integrator, DST accomplishes its mission by using proven, off-the-shelf hardware and software solutions when possible, and inventing effective hardware and software solutions when necessary.

Our professional multi-disciplined staff provides control, automation, and data collection systems to a wide range of industries: From pharmaceuticals to refineries; from water treatment to power-grid substations; from vibration-dampening systems for satellite launch vehicles to automated quality control systems for bottling lines—if processes, equipment, or industrial data flow need automating, DST Controls is the systems integrator to do it.

We purposely maintain this "broadband" experience and varied customer-base to not only keep our highly trained technical staff on their toes—and excited about coming to work each day—but also to shorten their learning-curve on new customer applications. This works effectively because...
  1. having so much experience already under our belts increases the likelihood that we will already be familiar with critical aspects of any new customers' situations; and
  2. having to regularly come-up-to-speed-quickly on new customer situations—and then immediately innovate solutions for them—at once tests, and strengthens, two of DST's core competencies: Learning & Innovating.

Moreover, DST's being at home in diverse environments also allows for occasional "cross-pollination" of solutions invented for one industry to be effectively applied in another. Hence, DST is often the "Go-to integrator" when a project's technical requirements may stymie other engineering groups.
