Control Engineering (P.E.)

While DST's core offer remains automated monitoring and control, our professional engineering staff is purposely diverse in their respective formal training (degrees) and previous work experience. DST's technical-team roster includes: control engineers, electrical engineers, mechanical engineers, chemical engineers, computer science engineers, and our "trained-in-the-trenches" Technicians. Yet, just as our wide-ranging customers are united by their common need to, in some way, monitor and control mass and data, so our multi-disciplined engineering staff is united in the meeting of that need.

DST's skill sets in the domains of machine, process, and industrial data automation include:
  • PLC & Computer programming
  • Industrial Networks and Plant Floor Communication
  • Custom Drivers (C++ and assembly language)
  • Database, ERP, & MRP
  • HMI, SCADA, and DCS
  • Variable Frequency Drives and Motion Control
  • Bar Code and Radio Frequency Tags
  • Control Panel Design (UL, CE, CSA)
  • Vision Inspection
