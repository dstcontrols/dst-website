Installation, Start-up, and Training

DST's electrical contractor's license number is CA# 466808. Hence we can take full responsibility for installation as well as all other phases of an automation project. Completed DST-projects—with installation—include: drum conveyor systems in New Jersey; bottling-line quality control systems in Texas; and hazardous gas control in Taiwan.
Where geography, project-size, customer preferences, or other issues require it, we will also happily contract with local mechanical or electrical contractors to provide installation services.

When appropriate, we will also act as a "sub" to electrical or mechanical contractors and assist those entities in whatever technical aspects of installation and start-up they wish to off-load.

Training:
DST regularly develops and delivers training programs for industrial processes and systems that were provided by DST or that were provided by others. Subjects include Operations, Maintenance, Trouble-shooting, and Safety.
