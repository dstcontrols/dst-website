Technical and Specification Writing

DST's technical and specification writing services include...
  • Fully engineered Control System bid packages
  • Functional Specifications
  • Operations Manuals
  • Maintenance Manuals
  • Training Manuals
  • Safety Manuals
  • Testing Procedures
  • Standard Operating Procedures
  • Design Specifications
  • Instrument Data Sheets
  • P&IDs (Process and Instrumentation Diagrams)
DST regularly assists its customers and other engineering companies in writing specifications for control and monitoring applications. This can be as simple a situation as just specifying a few hardware components accompanied by a wiring diagram, or it can comprise an entire enterprise-wide automation strategy and implementation plan: including production-related IT communications, SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition), BMS (Building Management Systems), and process and machine monitoring and control.
