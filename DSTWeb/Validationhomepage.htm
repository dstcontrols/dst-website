VALIDATION AND REGULATORY SERVICES

 

DST Controls offers clients a full range of validation and compliance programs, including:

  • process, facilities, equipment and analytical apparatus validation services

  • cGMP compliance and technical consulting

  • training programs

  • computer systems validation

 

These services are offered to the pharmaceutical, biotech, bulk pharmaceutical chemical, medical device, veterinary, and biologic industries. Our engineers have unparalleled regulatory expertise, with experience with FDA industry regulatory affairs and quality assurance issues. They work with clients in all areas of regulated industry, anywhere in the world, and have direct contact with FDA, MCA, PIC, and other international regulatory agency personnel.

cGMP/Regulatory Compliance 

Audits to review the requirements of cGMP are completed by DST Controls 
during design, engineering and construction of facilities for licensed manufacturing.

These audits focus on: 
  • Segregation
  • Containment and Isolation
  • Flow of Materials, Personnel, Product, Glassware and Waste
  • HVAC Zoning, Pressurization and Filtration 
  • Clean Utilities 
  • Drain and Exhaust Systems 
  • Aseptic Processing 
  • Cleaning and Decontamination 
  • Spill Containment 
  • Validatability 
  • Documentation 
 
cGMP/Validation 

DST is able to provide all or part of the following validation program management 
services to complement the client's in house capabilities:
  • Validation Document Preparation and Execution
  • Integrate the validation activities into the design and construction schedule
  • Maintain the schedule coordinating efforts of the team in order to meet time and budget goals.
  • Design and Implement Documentation Systems
  • Design and Implement Metrology Programs

 
DST provides complete validation of facilities, operating utilities, processing equipment and process.

A full validation program includes a Master Validation Plan consisting of:

  • Summary of the philosophical approach and rationale
  • Summary of the methodology and techniques
  • Validation planning worksheet identifying individual tasks
  • Flow diagrams showing personnel, product and waste
  • List of available resources, internal and external
  • Resource leveling method schedule to project the time required for the project based on the available resources

DST combines the Master Validation Plan with the construction schedule to ensure that:

  • Validation is a focus of the total effort.
  • Documentation is available as necessary.
  • Validation documents are prepared concurrently with construction.
  • The overall time to complete validation is minimized.

Our Validation Protocol Preparation Service includes:

  • Installation Qualifications
  • Operational Qualifications
  • Performance Qualifications
  • Validation Protocol Field Execution/Data Collection
  • Final Reports
