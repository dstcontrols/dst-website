DST CONTROLS is an electrical engineering firm, specializing in control systems integration. We serve our customers around the world with a staff of electrical, chemical, and mechanical engineers, PEs, and engineering technicians. We provide services from emergency response, to control system design, to turnkey project management. We have a long and successful history in the process industries with an impressive list of repeat customers.

In addition to this "soft" side of DST, we maintain a modern, 14,000-sq. ft. manufacturing center. This division produces high volume OEM subassemblies, cables, control panels, motor control center modifications, and other production work for customers on five continents.

Newest to DST's offering is a line of products under the unifying banner of ClearControlsTM. We've taken our 25 years of controls systems design and created several exciting new products available for sale "unbundled" from our traditional engineering services business. These products are the culmination of years of experience conceiving, designing, engineering, and building reliable control systems. Whether you need to link a control system to an information system, make your operator more productive, or gather data on a handheld linked to the server, you can tap into a wealth of the latest technologies right here.
