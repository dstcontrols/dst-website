DST and 21st Century Innovation

 

One of the things about which I am very proud is the team spirit we have at DST. A lot of CEOs talk of the great company they have. It is not possible to have a great company without great people. Each and every one of us contributes to the overall success of DST. Obviously, if one does not contribute to that success, the group will ask you to leave. Our employees are here because they have demonstrated the skill and desire to be part of the DSTeam.

 

Our 26-year history has established DST as an honorable and skillful team of dedicated people. We are using that history to launch our assault on the 21st century. We are leveraging our vast experience in the realm of industrial automation to create products for the factory of the future. Since our credibility comes from the engineering projects we have done, we will continue to work in that arena. To address needs not currently met by traditional suppliers, we have introduced new software and hardware products under the ClearControls trade name. We will continue to identify product areas where we can offer innovative solutions. 

 

Engineering services--Engineered Products--Turn-key solutions for factory automation and data collection. All of these, delivered with our usual high quality, will continue our success in 2001.

 

At DST, we encourage our employees to grow as we grow. We help them identify the qualities they need to be successful at DST. First and foremost they must be able to embrace the future while enjoying the present. The entire cadre at DST shares a vision of our future company while contributing to its success today. We must care about the expectations our clients have for our products and services. Our attention to details and quality are very important. We must have a team spirit. We want strong individuals working together as a team to accomplish our purpose.

 

What is this purpose? To profitably develop unique and innovative hardware and software solutions for industrial process data monitoring and control applications to improve the productivity of our clients.

 

What does all this mean to our employees?---A stable work environment where the team member can grow professionally by performing interesting work and training for the future—a future that includes the family and the team. We want to pay the best wages to the best people and provide benefits that allow each individual the chance to build for the future while enjoying today.

 

William P. Southard

June 26, 2001
