Food and Beverage

DST's combined experience with price-competitive municipal projects and zero (mistake) tolerance in the semiconductor and biotech industries makes DST an ideal control and monitoring systems integrator to the Food & Beverage industries.
DST's Food & Beverage experience includes...
  • Beer:   Automated keg filling line with PLC control, computer interface to host computer.
  • Wine:   Bottling line conveyor system with automated packaging using PLC control with computer interface for master scheduling.
  • Chocolate:   Energy Management System for chocolate agitators and sequence control.
  • Meat Packing:   Automated ground beef processing system with variable speed drives and programmable controls.
  • Peaches:   Aseptic fruit packaging equipment monitoring and control.
  • Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil:   Automatic batching system with heat recovery.
  • Meat Processing:   Product refrigeration and conveying.
  • Mustard:   Batch process control with HMI and report generation.
  • Sugar:   HMI / PLC control of sugar manufacturing melt house.
  • Ketchup:   Bottling Line automation with large integrated PLC, data acquisition and report generation.
  • Protein conversion process:   Automation using PLC's and instrumentation.
  • Chocolate Molding & Packaging:   PLC upgrade from OEM's dedicated (and out-dated!) control system.
  • Prune-pitting system:   Safety controls
  • Salad dressing blending facility:   PLC-based recipe control, blending, and bottling.
