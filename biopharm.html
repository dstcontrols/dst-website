Life Sciences / Pharmaceutical Manufacturing

DST Controls' BioPharm experience spans 30 years and our BioPharm customers span the North American continent. DST's control and monitoring systems are the core of numerous building management systems (BMS), facility control systems, process control systems, batch control systems, Clean In Place (CIP) systems, packaging systems, and safety systems. Many of these projects included DST Installation and Validation services as well.

DST's Validation Department is fully versed in GAMP - 5 and 21 CFR Part 11 compliance and can generate, as well as execute, virtually any validation or documentation protocols.
