Team Members Wanted!

DST CONTROLS (est. 1975), San Francisco Bay Area's preeminent Control Systems Integrator, is currently seeking to fill the following positions:

Software Engineer

Engineer will create applications and subsets of applications used in industrial control and information systems applications including graphical interfaces, communication drivers, database archive and retrieval tools, and structured reporting documentation . As work is often subject to DISA certification, candidate will be subject to random drug testing.

Job Requirements:
  • Candidate must have BS in Computer Science and/or Computer Engineering
  • Ability to creatively and independently identify problems and propose solutions
  • Dedication and self-motivation

The technical skills required for this position include demonstrable proficiency with the following:

  • Visual Basic programming
  • C programming
  • Object-Oriented programming

Highly desirable skills include:

  • SQL Server programming
  • Java programming
  • C++ programming
  • Relational Database
  • SQL and stored procedures and COM/DCOM

Control Systems/Project Engineer

Engineer will fulfill client requirements for industrial control and information systems applications including control system design, hardware and software selection, drawings, PLC and HMI programming, network configuration and system start-up. As work is often subject to DISA certification, candidate will be subject to random drug testing.

Job Requirements:

  • Candidate must have BS in Electrical, Mechanical, or Computer Science/Computer Engineering
  • Four (4) yrs experience in PLC and SCADA based industrial control
  • Ability to creatively and independently identify problems and propose solutions
  • Dedication and self-motivation

The technical skills required for this position include demonstrable proficiency with the following:

  • PLC Programming
  • HMI Screen and Database Development
  • System Commissioning and Troubleshooting

Materials Manager

Represents the needs of the Engineering and Manufacturing Directors to suppliers regarding what is needed and when. Will manage ordering, planning, forecasting, and control of all inventory. Work with all levels of personnel (hourly, supervisory, and engineering) resolving issues, tracking material availability, and advising on material usage. Design and implement progressive management processes and systems including vendor selection and performance evaluation. Supervise hourly Procurement Planners, Purchasing Associates, Materials Associates, and Material Expediters. Promote customer satisfaction and develop positive customer relationships.  

Successful candidate will have:

  • Materials management experience

  • Customer service/contract experience

  • Strong computer skills

  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, influencing, and organizational integration skills

  • MRP, APICS experience preferred

  • Accomplishments which reflect teamwork, integrity, accountability, and speed
