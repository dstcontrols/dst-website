The scope of work we undertake is client-driven.

That is, at the discretion of our clients, we will deliver an entire turnkey control system or, in concert with our clients' in-house people or other sub-contractors, we will provide only those phases or sub-systems of a project that they wish to out-source.

Our business arrangements are also tailored to fit our clients' preferences.

Available as needed are:

  • Fixed price contract
  • Time and Materials (T&M) contracts
  • Bids to specifications
  • Design/Build contracts
  • DST-written specifications
  • Bonding
  • Non-disclosure agreements
  • Insurance certification
  • Drug-test certification
