In the field of industrial automation, DST's seasoned engineers and technicians have 'in-the-trenches' experience with every major brand and type of control hardware, software, and operating system.

DST's resident skill sets are also continually upgraded through factory sponsored and professional education programs.

Technologies integrated include:

  • Computer and Programmable Logic Control (PLC)
  • Variable Speed Drive (VSD)
  • Human Machine Interface (HMI)
  • Bar Code, RF Tag, and Intelligent Sensors
  • Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA)
  • Distributed Control Systems (DCS)
  • Control Communications: WAN/LAN and custom driver development
  • Motion Control
  • Batch Control
  • Process Control
  • Machine Automation
  • Telemetry

The net result is an extensive and expanding satisfied-client reference list.
