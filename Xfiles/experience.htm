Broad experience equals technical excellence

DST values the diversity of its client-base to the point of having consciously avoided becoming a 'niche player,' in spite of periodic enticements from the market place to do so.

Our eclectic industry-focus has ensured DST's long term financial stability, and has produced for our customers a truly expert DST engineering corps, enlightened and hardened by their rigorous cross-industry training.

With literally thousands of DST systems installed throughout a plethora of industries world-wide, our engineers often employ elements of control solutions engineered for one industry in another.

Such 'cross-pollination' can side-step the expense and time consumption of 're-inventing the wheel.'

However, DST fully appreciates the technical, ethical, and legal distinctions between 'bringing previous experience to a new project' and transference of proprietary information. Hence, our clients' rights and expectations in this area govern our actions at all times.
