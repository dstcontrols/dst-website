DST Controls has been an electrical engineering firm specializing in automated controls and data acquisition systems since its founding in 1975.

As a Controls Systems Integrator, DST's mission is the maximization of its clients' productivity and cost efficiency.

Our professional staff of electrical, mechanical, and software engineers -- in conjunction with our technical writers and drafts people -- accomplish this promptly, reliably, and economically through the application of proven automation control technologies.

Our goal is simple:

"Exceed our clients' expectations to the point that we would be their first choice for their next project."
