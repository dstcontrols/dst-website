DST CONTROLS Announces eOS-Model 600

BENICIA, CA, February 2, 2001 - DST Controls, a Microsoft Windows® Embedded Partner, has released the world’s first operator interface family to support running either the Windows Embedded or full desktop operating systems. Dubbed the eOS-600 Series for "embedded operating system", these bright, color-touch displays range in size from six to 21 inches diagonal measure. For the first time, operator interface users can invest in developing their application without risk the hardware that it runs on will become obsolete. (read more)

DST CONTROLS Appointed Embedded Partner

       BENICIA, CA - (Nov 15, 2000) - DST CONTROLS announced it has received recognition as a both a Systems Integrator and OEM developer for Microsoft for using their Embedded Operating system products. Joining the likes of Rockwell, Symbol, and Siemens, DST has committed to deploying products and offering integration services for products utilizing this factory floor ready O/S.

DST CONTROLS Introduces ClearControlsTM

DST CONTROLS NAMED AS ONE OF REGIONS FASTEST GROWING EXPORTERS

       BENICIA, CA - (June 11, 1999) - DST CONTROLS was recognized by SF Business Times for posting the highest gains in exporting revenue from 1996 to 1998. Joining the ranks of other well known Bay Area based companies such as Robert Mondavi, PowerBar, Honeywell-Measurex, and JR Schneider, DST received this recognition from the Business Times and Database Publishing, a southern California research firm.

       DST has taken advantage of its perch on the eastern edge of the Pacific Rim to launch many of its products westward into Asia. Despite the current Asian economic difficulties, demand for DST's semiconductor related specialty gas cabinet controllers and engineered life safety systems has remained strong. The SF Business Times recognized DST's contribution to the roughly $100 billion in exports and imports which pass through the San Francisco US Customs District. It is believed this trade directly and indirectly helps to support an estimated 1.5 million jobs in the Bay Area.

       The list of companies recognized in the annual report covered in a diverse spectrum of businesses including shipping scrap metal, finished semiconductors, and food products. Destinations included Poland and Panama as well as Mexico and Malaysia. DST CONTROLS CEO William P Southard said of the announcement "I am pleased to see our company's name listed among other Bay Area superstars. But, I am more pleased that we have been able to achieve this recognition while maintaining our commitment to serving our domestic clients."

       DST CONTROLS is the largest independent control systems integrator in California. Headquartered in Benicia, California, this electrical engineering firm has specialized in automated controls and data acquisition systems since its founding in 1975. They maintain a professional staff of electrical, mechanical, and software engineers -- who in conjunction with technical writers and drafts people -- promptly, reliably, and economically deliver automated control systems.
